Alice in Wonderland

Alice's Adventures in Wonderland (also known as Alice in Wonderland) is an 1865 novel written by English author Charles Lutwidge Dodgson who is better known by his pseudonym Lewis Carroll.

I love the story of Aice in Wonderland. It's so visually charched even when there as no illustrations attached and it'll make the imagination of the reader fly. So I was more than happy when we were assigned in mu my school to make illustrations for the sroty. I wanted to step out of my comfort zone and do something different. Something I haven't done before. I ended up mixing painting and yarn among some other elements (such as fur and rhinestones).
